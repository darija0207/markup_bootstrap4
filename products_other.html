    Purified Lipids

    Purified Lipids

    Category Category 2 Category 3 Category 4 Category 5

    Our serum products are of the highest quality, and our processes have been perfected over our 40 year corporate history. Our product specifications and rigorous quality assurance checks ensure lot to lot consistency and a high customer acceptance rate. Processing steps include analyte specific screening, defibrination, diafiltration in isotonic buffer, depth filtration, and 0.22 micron final filtration. 

    • Plasma is collected from US FDA licensed blood collection facilities, with complete donor traceability; donor units are tested and found to be negative for all FDA required pathogens.
    • Processing performed in a cGMP, FDA‑registered, ISO 13485‑certified facility Lot sizes from 1L to 2000L
    • Custom products routinely added to catalog based on customer specifications.

    Cone Bioproducts is a third generation family business centered on developing and bringing to market human plasma and serum products.
