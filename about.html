    Who We Are

    Cone Bioproducts is a third generation family business founded by former medical technologist Robert O. Cone Jr. in 1970 to produce diagnostic test kit components including antisera, quality control products, and proficiency testing specimens. Joined by his son Bill, the Cone family were visionaries in the industry, and developed a significant amount of technological and production knowledge still utilized to this day.

    What Makes Us Different

    Cone Bioproducts is dedicated to adding exceptional value to its products by meeting or exceeding customer expectations in the areas of regulatory compliance, customer support, product quality, supply availability, and lot-to-lot consistency.
