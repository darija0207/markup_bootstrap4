    Defibrinated Human Serums

    Defibrinated Human Serum is manufactured from human plasma by adding chemicals that cause the plasma to clot. The clot is subsequently removed from the plasma, creating human serum.

    Synonyms: Defibrinated Human Plasma, Normal Human Serum, Human Basematrix.
