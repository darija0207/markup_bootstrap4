    Our Process

    Design

    • Safety and effectiveness of product
    • Basematrix selection
    • Analyte and Target Levels, # Levels, Stabilizers, Packaging
    • Form: Liquid or Lyophilized

    Pilot Production

    • 1st Pilot – Confirm specifications achievable
    • 2nd Pilot – Use approved manufacturing instructions, Duplicate 1st Pilot
    • 3rd Pilot – Bulk Lot

    Regulatory
    Submission

    • 510K/CE Mark Approval sought as needed
    • Compile data from 3 Pilots and submit for approval 

    Bulk Production

    • Continued performance testing including real-time stability 

    Change Request

    Determine if change is:
    • Feasible 
    • Requires design changes
    • Validated in pilot production 
    • Requires new regulatory submission
    • Approval by customer
