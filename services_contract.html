    Lyophilization

    Lyophilization

    Two Hull Lyophilization Devices are available on site, with 40, and 120 Liter ice capacities. Our lyophilization cycles are able to have full customization of parameters, including: Temperature, vacuum pressure, time, containers and more. Leveraging our experience, we can recommend a lyophilization cycle, develop a custom cycle, or follow our customers' pre-existing specifications. 

    Testing & Screening

    Testing & Screening

    Cone Bioproducts offers extensive in-house testing for chemistry, immunology, immunoassay, serology and HbA1c analytes. Testing platforms include but are not limited to Abaxis, Abbott, Mindray, Roche, Siemens, and Tosoh analyzers.

    Open platform R&D analyzers provide us the capability to program and run customer provided tests at our facility. Cone Bioproducts regularly characterizes several hundred liter batches of plasma for use in manufacturing and provides screened material as a service to other manufacturers
